Kanye West is a multi-talented artist and entrepreneur known for reshaping music, fashion, and culture. Starting as a producer, he rose to fame with critically acclaimed albums like The College Dropout (2004) and My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy (2010). His music blends innovation and emotion, while his Yeezy brand revolutionized sneaker culture and fashion. Despite controversies surrounding his public persona and views, West remains one of the most influential figures in entertainment and culture.

Playboi Carti

Playboi Carti's career took off with his self-titled mixtape in 2017, featuring the hit Magnolia. He followed it with the acclaimed album Die Lit (2018), blending minimalist beats and melodic flows. In 2020, Whole Lotta Red debuted at No. 1, showcasing a punk-inspired evolution in his sound. Known for his experimental style and influence, Carti remains a prominent figure in hip-hop.

Travis Scott's career began with mixtapes like Owl Pharaoh (2013) and Days Before Rodeo (2014), leading to his breakout album Rodeo (2015) featuring Antidote. His third album, Astroworld (2018), brought him global fame with the hit Sicko Mode. Known for his genre-blending sound and innovative collaborations, Scott remains a major force in modern hip-hop.
